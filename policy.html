Privacy Policy for Auto Scroll Extension

The Auto Scroll extension does not collect any user data. It only stores the user-specified URL of their local Komga comic reader to enable automatic scrolling on that specific website. No personal information or browsing data is collected or transmitted.

Support the Developer!

To help support the ongoing development and maintenance of the Auto Scroll extension, a "Support Auto Scroll on Ko-fi! ❤️" button is included in the extension's popup. Clicking this button will redirect you to the developer's Ko-fi profile at https://ko-fi.com/nuked, where you can make a voluntary donation.

Please note that your use of Ko-fi is subject to Ko-fi's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We do not receive any personally identifiable information (PII) from Ko-fi about donors, unless you choose to share that information with us directly (e.g., by sending us a message through Ko-fi).
